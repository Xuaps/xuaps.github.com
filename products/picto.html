Picto

Picto is a tool for alternative communication. Using pictograms, helps people with any kind of language disorders or impairments to communicate in a more effective way.

Picto is fully customizable. Including a preset gallery, it reaches its true potential when customized to the needs of the users. Besides, it is really easy to use: you just have to chose a picture and a sound.
